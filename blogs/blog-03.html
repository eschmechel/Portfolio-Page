Understanding SEO for Developers

May 7, 2025 • 6 min read

SEO is crucial for making your website discoverable. This post explains the basics of SEO, including meta tags, canonical URLs, sitemaps, and how to avoid common pitfalls.

Why SEO Matters

Good SEO practices help your site appear in search results, increasing visibility and traffic.

Key Elements

  • Meta tags: title, description, and keywords
  • Canonical URLs to prevent duplicate content
  • Sitemaps for search engine indexing

Common Mistakes

Avoid duplicate content, broken links, and missing alt text on images.
